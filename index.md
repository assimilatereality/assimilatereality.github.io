# *Assimilate Reality*

The Quest. What can it be but a search. The Holy Grail is Truth, Reality. It is to be feared, perhaps more than it is sought, for our minds cannot handle the onslaught of unfiltered reality. The minds of sentient beings alter incoming data by passing it through a filter, selecting and processing into a final reduced information awareness, that is, functional truth. They are effectively in a state of bias, that is, delusion. While necessary to function normally, it hides details that can enable greater understanding and actions. Hence the value potential of the Quest.

The truth of a thing can only be known through Direct Perception of its reality. To not have Truth is to be blinded, unable to understand reality, or what is real.

The field of data science offers tools that enable us to harness computing power that dwarfs what was possible only a few years ago. Using it, we can grapple with datasets in an attempt to remove layers of bias. This blog will document some of my contributions to the Quest.
